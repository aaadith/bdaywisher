Ever forgotten to wish a dear one on his/her birthday? Ever wished you could have sent a sweet mail with a little gift on the special occasion and repented the missed chance? Ever found it too difficult to keep track of so many birthdays and to wish people at the right time? bdaywisher to the rescue.With bdaywisher, all you need to do is add the birthday and email address of friends you have to wish..and sit back..and forget about it. bdaywisher would take care of sending a birthday-wish-mail to your friends on their birthday..year after year. Whats more, it even sends a small gift of your choosing as an attachment along with the mail. Who wouldn't like to get a birthday gift..exactly on their birthday...at the stroke of midnight...
